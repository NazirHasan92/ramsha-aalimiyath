Why purification is the FIRST chapter of fiqh

Every act of worship requires a state of purity:

  • 🕌 Salah
  • 🕋 Tawaf
  • 📖 Touching the Mushaf

The cause of that purity → water (or earth, when no water).

Imam-i-akram ne is liye paani ka bayaan sab se pehle rakha — kyunki har ibaadat ki bunyaad hai.

By the end of this lesson, you will:

  1. Define tahara in both senses (linguistic + juristic)
  2. List the 7 sources of pure water
  3. Distinguish the 5 types of water
  4. Apply the colour-taste-smell test
  5. Know the 4 su'r animal categories
  6. Apply the well rules

Rule 1 · الطَّهَارَة

الطَّهَارَة

tahara

= purification — a legal state of cleanliness

= paaki ki qaanooni haalat

Two senses of tahara

Linguistic

General cleanliness — physical OR spiritual.

"Paaki" — aam matlab. Allah Quran mein "soul ki paaki" ki baat bhi karte hain.

Juristic (fiqhi)

A precise legal state required for prayer.

Loci: body · clothes · prayer-area

Cause: clean water (or earth)

📿 Qur'an — Q 25:48

"He caused rain to descend from the heaven, to clean you therewith." (Q 25:48)

"Allah ne aasmaan se baarish naazil ki, taake tumhe us se paak kare."

🕊️ The Master Hadith

The Prophet (ﷺ) said:

"Water is pure and is not rendered impure by anything, except a thing that alters its colour, taste or smell."

3 things to check: colour · taste · smell

3 nishaaniyaan: rang · zaayqa · boo

This single hadith governs the ENTIRE chapter.

Rule 2 · The 5 Types of Water

Ask two separate questions about any water:

  1. Is it pure in itself? (kya khud paak hai?)
  2. Can it purify others? (kya doosron ko paak kar sakta hai?)

These are NOT the same question.

The 5 types — full table

TypePure?Purifying?Example
1 · mutlaq✅✅Tap water
2 · disliked✅✅ but dislikedCat's saucer
3 · musta'mal✅❌Wudu drippings
4 · najis❌❌Filthy water
5 · mashkuk✅❓Donkey su'r

Type 3 · The tricky one — musta'mal

Used water — water that has already done its job.

📌 Pure? ✅ Yes — it's not "dirty".

📌 Purifying? ❌ No — it can't lift ritual impurity.

✅ Can be used for…

Washing physical filth (urine, blood, wine) off clothes/body.

❌ Cannot be used for…

Wudu or ghusl.

Musta'mal paani: paak hai par "thakka hua" — wudu nahin, par kapdon se najasat dhone mein theek.

When does water become "used"?

"Right after it separates from the body."

📌 Water still on your hand → NOT yet musta'mal.

📌 Moment the drop falls off → NOW it's musta'mal.

Type 4 · Filthy water — the threshold

"Small still water" with filth in it → najis. Even if no trace appears.

Threshold:

  • Square: < 10 × 10 arms (≈ 5m × 5m)
  • Circular: circumference < 36 arms
  • 1 arm = fingertip to elbow ≈ 48 cm

Type 5 · Doubtful — donkey & mule

Water that a mule or donkey has drunk from.

The doubt is in its purifying capacity, not its purity.

The ruling — do BOTH:

1️⃣ Wudu with this water →

2️⃣ Then tayammum →

3️⃣ Then pray.

Donon karo — ek nahi to doosri kaam aa jaayegi. Yeh "ihtiyaat" ka asool hai.

Rule 3 · When water gets contaminated

Three separate tests:

  1. Mixed with a solid → does it still flow thin?
  2. Mixed with a liquid → "1-of-2" or "2-of-3" characteristics?
  3. Mixed with filth → small / large / running?

Mixed with a solid

✅ Still water

Saffron, leaves, fruit — water STILL FLOWS THIN.

Wudu OK even if colour changed.

❌ Not water

Cooked lentils, thickened broth — water LOST flow.

No wudu.

Solid mile to bahaav ka test: agar abhi bhi patli dhaar mein behta hai → paani.

🕊️ Hadith on lotus leaves

The Prophet (ﷺ) said about a man who fell from his camel and died:

"Wash him with water and lotus leaves, and shroud him in two garments."

— Bukhari 1268

Proof: leaves (solid) don't disqualify water as long as it still flows.

Rule 4 · السُّؤْر · Leftover water

السُّؤْر

su'r

= small-quantity water remaining after an animal has drunk

= jhoota paani — jaanwar ke peene ke baad bacha hua

📌 River/ocean water is NEVER su'r — only small quantities count.

The 4 categories

1 · Pure ✅

Human · Horse

Halal-meat animals (camel, cow, sheep, goat)

2 · Filthy ❌

Dog · Pig

Predatory LAND animal (wolf, fox, cheetah)

3 · Disliked 😐

Cat · Stray chicken

Predatory BIRD (eagle, falcon)

House rat / snake

4 · Doubtful ❓

Mule · Donkey

→ wudu + tayammum + pray

The most important nuance ⚠️

🐺 Predatory LAND animal

Drinks with TONGUE — wet with saliva (impure).

→ Leftover FILTHY

🦅 Predatory BIRD

Drinks with BEAK — bone, which is pure.

→ Leftover only DISLIKED

Yeh chapter ka sab se aham distinction — yaad rakhna.

About the dog 🐕

Only the dog's saliva & tongue make water filthy.

The dog's body otherwise is pure.

If a dog jumps in a well, exits alive, and its tongue didn't touch the water →

The water is STILL PURE.

Kutte ke baare mein loag ghalat samajhte hain — sab kuch napaak nahin. Sirf lu'aab/zubaan napaak hai.

Rule 5 · الْآبَار · Wells

Why wells get their own section: a contaminated well was a public crisis in the classical Muslim community.

Default: a well is "small water" — so contamination is serious.

Imam-i-akram ne wells par alag tafseel di — kyunki sab log isi se peeyte the.

Tier A — ALL water must be removed

  1. Any impurity in a small well — even a drop of blood
  2. Pig falls in (even alive) — all parts of pig are impure
  3. Dog · sheep · human DIES in the well
  4. Any BLOATED dead animal — even small

Precedent: when a man died in Zamzam, Ibn Abbas and Ibn al-Zubair (RA) ordered all water withdrawn — many Companions were present, no objection.

Tier B — Bucket counts (can't drain it all)

What diedBuckets
Bloated animal200
Chicken · cat40
Rat · similar20

Buckets = average size, usually the well's own bucket.

These numbers come from Imam Abu Yusuf & Imam Hasan's ijtihad — not directly from Qur'an/Hadith.

Tier C — Things that do NOT ruin well water

  1. Small amount of livestock dung (camel, sheep, cow…)
  2. Droppings of halal birds (pigeon, sparrow)
  3. Death of bloodless creatures (fish, frog, mosquito, fly, wasp, scorpion, aquatic creatures)
  4. Human / halal animal / mule / donkey falling in BUT exiting ALIVE

🕊️ The pigeon hadith

The Prophet (ﷺ) thanked the pigeon:

"Verily, it nested on the door of the cave until I was safe, and may Allah reward it with the masjid as its home."

— Tabarani 20/443

And Ibn Mas'ud (RA) wiped away bird droppings with his hand.

→ This is WHY halal-bird droppings don't ruin well water.

Unknown duration rule

Dead animal found, nobody knows when it fell in:

Not bloated

Water ruled impure for 1 day & 1 night

→ Repeat 1 day+night of prayer

Bloated

Water ruled impure for 3 days & 3 nights

→ Repeat 3 days+nights of prayer

Exception: if water was only used to wash filthy clothes, just wash them again — no prayer repetition.

💎 The big insight

"Pure" and "purifying" are two different things.

A water can be pure but spent. (Type 3 · musta'mal)

A water can be pure but disliked. (Type 2 · cat su'r)

A water can be pure but doubtful. (Type 5 · donkey su'r)
