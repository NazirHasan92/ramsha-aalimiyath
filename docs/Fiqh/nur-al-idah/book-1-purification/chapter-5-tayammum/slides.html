Last time: wudū & ghusl — made of water

But what if there is no water — or water is there but you cannot use it?

Today: tayammum — the DRY substitute, made of EARTH.

"And if you find no water, then take for yourselves clean sand or earth and rub therewith your faces and hands. Allah does not wish to place you in difficulty."

— al-Māʾidah 5:6 (book's footnote: "al-Māʾidah 7")

Islam tumhe ibaadat se bahar nahin karta — yeh chapter Allah ki aasaani hai.

Three keys to the whole chapter

1 · Badal
a substitute — ON with a valid excuse, OFF the moment water returns
2 · Niyyah is a condition
soil purifies only when intended (water purifies by its essence)
3 · Two strikes
face + arms to the elbows, with the whole hand

Learn these first — almost every ruling turns on one of them.

Teen chaabiyaan — inse poora chapter khud khul jaata hai.

Rule 1 · When tayammum is allowed

You need a valid excuse — two kinds of "absence" of water:

A · Real absence
no water within ~1 mile / not found — search first (bird, fruit tree, someone to ask)
B · Legal absence
water there, can't use it: illness · cold · enemy/beast · thirst · no rope/bucket

Tayammum koi shortcut nahin — sirf majboori mein. Pehle paani dhoondo.

Fear of missing a prayer?

✓ Janāza & Eid
tayammum allowed — these cannot be made up
✗ Friday / obligatory time
NOT a reason — Friday → ẓuhr; the obligatory → qaḍā

Ibn ʿAbbās: "If the funeral prayer came and you feared you would miss it, pray with tayammum."

— Ibn ʿAdī, al-Kāmil 7/182

Janāza/Eid chhootne ka dar → jaayiz. Jumu'ah/farḍ-waqt → nahin.

Rule 2 · What & how — the act

Niyyah
a condition (not just a sunna) — soil purifies only when intended
Pure earth
soil · sand · stone · lime · clay
(NOT wood/metal/gold/ash)
Two strikes
face + arms to the elbows, whole hand

"Tayammum is two strikes, a strike for the face and a strike for the arms to his elbows."

— Hakim, al-Mustadrak 1/287

Niyyah shart · jins-e-zameen · do darb: chehra + baazu kohniyon tak.

The 2 pillars

Strip everything away — tayammum is just:

① wipe the FACE  +  ② wipe the ARMS to the elbows

Neglect one → invalid.

Also: clear the path first — ḥayḍ/nifās/ḥadath must end; remove wax or grease from the skin.

Arkan = 2: chehra + baazu (kohni samet).

Rule 3 · The complete tayammum — 7 sunan

  1. Bismillah at the start
  2. Order (tartīb) — face before arms
  3. No pausing (muwālāt)
  4. Rub the palms forward on the soil
  5. Rub them backward
  6. Shake the hands
  7. Spread the fingers

Tareeqa: Bismillah → tartīb → muwālāt → aage → peeche → jhaad → ungliyaan khuli (Daraqutni 1/179).

Rule 4 · How a tayammum lives

Delay it
hope of water → mandūb; promised water (close) → wājib
Request water
seek ~400 steps if near; ask a companion
Many prayers
ONE tayammum → many farḍ + nafl; valid before the time

Ḥanafī: one tayammum, many prayers — "pure until broken," like wudū.

Ek tayammum se jitni chaaho namazein. (Shāfiʿī: har farḍ ke liye naya.)

Rule 5 · What ends it — the mirror

① Like wudū
everything that breaks wudū (the whole Ch 3b list)
② The unique one
the return of usable water (or the excuse ending)

Uzr khatam → tayammum khatam.

Do naaqis: jo wudū ko tore + paani mil jaana. Paani aate hi tayammum khatam.

Recap

ALLOWED when:
no usable water — real absence (search first) or legal absence (illness/cold/enemy/thirst/no-rope); fear of missing Janāza/Eid
The act:
niyyah (a condition) · pure earth · two strikes · face + arms to elbows

2 pillars (face + arms) · 7 sunan · one tayammum → many prayers.

Ends with: wudū-breakers + water returning.

Tayammum = "mitti ka wudū/ghusl" — sirf tab tak jab tak paani door.
