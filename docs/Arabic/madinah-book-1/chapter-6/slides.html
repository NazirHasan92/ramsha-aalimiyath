Today: the feminine — the ة that runs through everything.

Rule 1 · التَّأْنِيث — the ة marker

Arabic has two genders — no "it".

مُذَكَّر · masculine → هَذَا / هُوَ
مُؤَنَّث · feminine → هَذِهِ / هِيَ

The switch — add ة

طَبِيب  +  ة  =  طَبِيبَة

"male doctor → female doctor"

مُسْلِم → مُسْلِمَة
مُهَنْدِس → مُهَنْدِسَة
كَبِير → كَبِيرَة

Mardaana lafz le lo, aakhir mein ة laga do.

⚠️ Urdu gender will trick you

Urdu gender does not match Arabic.

  • kitaab — feminine in Urdu, but كِتَابٌ is masculine in Arabic (هَذَا, هُوَ).

Urdu wali gender bhool jao — Arabic mein ة dekho ya yaad karo.

Rule 2 · Agreement — the ة flows through

Feminine subject → feminine describing words.

هَذِهِ دَرَّاجَةٌ جَدِيدَةٌ

hādhihi darrājatun jadīdatun — "this is a new bicycle"

Three feminine markers in a row · teen ة ek line mein

From the lesson

أَهِيَ مُهَنْدِسَةٌ ؟ — لَا، هِيَ طَبِيبَةٌ
"Is she an engineer? — No, she is a doctor."
هَذِهِ سَاعَةُ عَلِيٍّ. هِيَ جَمِيلَةٌ جِدًّا
"This is Ali's watch. It is very beautiful."

⚠️ Don't forget the ة on the adjective

✅ هِيَ طَبِيبَةٌ  ·  ❌ هِيَ طَبِيبٌ

✅ سَيَّارَةٌ جَدِيدَةٌ  ·  ❌ سَيَّارَةٌ جَدِيدٌ

Ek switch dab gaya — poori line roshan honi chahiye.

Rule 3 · Feminine without a ة

Most feminine words have ة — but a few don't.

In this lesson: the body parts that come in pairs.

Jodi-wale ange — bina ة ke feminine.

Two columns to memorise

هَذَا (masculine)

أَنْفٌ (nose)
فَمٌ (mouth)

هَذِهِ (feminine, no ة)

عَيْنٌ (eye) · أُذُنٌ (ear)
يَدٌ (hand) · رِجْلٌ (leg)

Hook: two eyes, two ears, two hands, two feet — all feminine.

The "obvious" ones too

Family words are also feminine without a ة:

بِنْتٌ · أُخْتٌ · أُمٌّ

"daughter/girl · sister · mother"

Yeh aasaan hain — aurat ke liye hain, isliye feminine.

Rule 4 · The feminine in iḍāfa & "Whose?"

Nothing new — old machinery, feminine nouns.

First noun stays bare: no الـ, no tanween → just ـةُ.

Feminine iḍāfa

بِنْتُ يَاسِرٍYasir's daughter
أُخْتُ الْمُهَنْدِسِthe engineer's sister
سَيَّارَةُ الْمُدِيرِthe director's car
بَقَرَةُ الْفَلَّاحِthe farmer's cow

"Whose?" about a feminine thing

سَيَّارَةُ مَنْ هَذِهِ ؟
⬇️
هَذِهِ سَيَّارَةُ الْمُدِيرِ

"Whose car is this? — This is the director's car."

⚠️ Keep the iḍāfa rules

✅ سَيَّارَةُ الْمُدِيرِ

❌ السَّيَّارَةُ الْمُدِيرِ   ❌ سَيَّارَةٌ الْمُدِيرِ

Pehla lafz — chahe feminine ho — bare rehta hai.
