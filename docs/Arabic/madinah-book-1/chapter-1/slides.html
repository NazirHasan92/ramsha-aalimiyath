Rule 1 · The word for "this"

هَذَا

haadhaa

= "this is..." (for a nearby thing)

= "yeh hai..." (kisi qareeb cheez ki taraf ishaara)

Two things to remember

  1. The ا is silent.
    Say "haadhaa" — not "ha-aa-dhaa".
    ا silent hai — "aa" ki awaaz beech mein, aakhir mein nahin
  2. Use it for masculine things only.
    (Feminine "this" comes later.)
    Sirf mardaana cheezon ke liye — feminine baad mein

Example

هَذَا بَيْتٌ

haadhaa baytun

"This is a house"

"Yeh ek ghar hai"

Rule 2 · Arabic has NO word for "is"

In English: "This is a house" (3 words)

In Urdu: "Yeh ek ghar hai" (4 words)

In Arabic: هَذَا بَيْتٌ (only 2 words!)

The silence between the words IS the "is".

Khaali jagah hi "hai" ka kaam karti hai — koi extra lafz nahin.

Two words side by side

هَذَا + بَيْتٌ

هَذَا = "this"   بَيْتٌ = "a house"

= "This is a house" · "Yeh ek ghar hai"

Notice the little -un ending: baytun, qalamun — just copy the sound.

Har lafz ke aakhir mein "-un" ki awaaz — bas naqal karo.

Rule 3 · "What is this?" / "Who is this?"

Two question words — one for things, one for people.

Do sawaali lafz — ek cheezon ke liye, ek logon ke liye.

Things vs People

🔹 For THINGS

مَا هَذَا؟

maa haadhaa?

"What is this?" · yeh kya hai?

🔸 For PEOPLE

مَنْ هَذَا؟

man haadhaa?

"Who is this?" · yeh kaun hai?

مَا = kya (cheez) · مَنْ = kaun (insaan)

Ask & answer

مَا هَذَا؟ — هَذَا نَجْمٌ

"What is this? — This is a star." · "Yeh kya hai? — Yeh ek sitara hai."

مَنْ هَذَا؟ — هَذَا طَبِيبٌ

"Who is this? — This is a doctor." · "Yeh kaun hai? — Yeh ek doctor hai."

Rule 4 · Asking a yes/no question

Just add أَ in front!

(pronounced "a-")

Bas "a-" jumle ke shuru mein lagao — sawal ban gaya.

Statement → Question

هَذَا بَيْتٌ

"This is a house" · "Yeh ek ghar hai"

⬇️
أَهَذَا بَيْتٌ ؟

"Is this a house?" · "Kya yeh ek ghar hai?"

Answer with...

نَعَمْ

naʿam

YES

haan

لَا

laa

NO

nahin

Full Q&A example

أَهَذَا كَلْبٌ ؟

"Is this a dog?" · "Kya yeh ek kutta hai?"

لَا ، هَذَا قِطٌّ

"No, this is a cat." · "Nahin, yeh ek billi hai."

📿 Connect to the Qur'an

You can already recognise Chapter 1's patterns in the Qur'an!

Aap Chapter 1 ke jumle Qur'an mein pehchaan sakte ho!

Surah Al-Anʿām 6:76

قَالَ هَٰذَا رَبِّي

"He said: This is my Lord." (Prophet Ibrāhīm)

"Us ne kaha: yeh mera Rabb hai."

There's today's word هَذَا = "this"!

Surah Yūsuf 12:31

مَا هَٰذَا بَشَرًا

"This is no mortal man!"

"Yeh koi insaan nahin hai!"

Today's question words مَا هَذَا — "what is this?"

Surah Al-Furqān 25:41

أَهَٰذَا الَّذِي بَعَثَ اللَّهُ رَسُولًا

"Is this the one whom Allah has sent as a messenger?"

"Kya yeh wohi hai jise Allah ne rasool bana kar bheja?"

The question particle أَ + هَذَا = "is this…?"
